Top places to visit in Ukraine

Attractions

For Nature Admirers

Aktove canyon is one of the most unique places in Ukraine. At sunrises, the canyon rocks` color turns to pink... More about the canyon

Pink lake has earned fame thanks to its unusual pink colour, caused by blossoming of water plants... About the lake

Approximately 230 km of passageways have been mapped within Optymistychna Pechera. As a result, it is the longest cave in Eurasia... More about the cave

For Architecture Lovers

The facades of Pidhirtsi Castle have obvious "French look", and one can witness a wonderful view from the windows: the area of tens of kilometres is visible to the north... More about the castle

Mariyinsky Palace is the official ceremonial residence of the President of Ukraine. The Splendorous Baroque building was constructed for Empress Elizabeth Petrovna... About the palace

Residence of Bukovyna and Dalmatia Metropolitans resembles a medieval palace. It consists of three united buildings: seminary, monastery and the main building... Residence in Chernivtsi

Annunciation Cathedral rivets attention by its original forms and uncommon striped patterns. Built in Russian and Byzantine style, it`s recognized as a monument of the 20th century... More about the cathedral

House with Chimaeras is an amazing brick building in the Art Nouveau style. It got its name because of the sculptural decoration... House of Chimaeras

For History Fans

The Rozumovskyi Palace was a residence of the last hetman of the Cossack Hetmanate. It`s the only masterpiece of Charles Cameron in Ukraine... The Rozumovskyi Palace

Khortytsia is the largest island in the River Dnipro. The major part of the reserve covers the Cossack Museum that includes the Cossack horse show... More about Khortytsia

The Verkhovna Rada is the parliament of Ukraine... About the parliament
